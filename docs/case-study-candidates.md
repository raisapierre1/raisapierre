## How Many Projects Per Employer?

Based on industry research:

- **1 strong case study per employer is ideal** — depth over breadth
- Senior portfolios should have **3-5 total case studies**, not 10-15 thin ones
- Each case study should take 5-10 minutes to read thoroughly
- For Betsson (3.5 years), you could do **2 case studies** if the projects are distinct enough (e.g., Sportsbook redesign + Design System unification)
- Never pad with weak projects — better to have 3 excellent ones than 5 mediocre ones

**Rule of thumb from research:**
> Hiring managers look at 2-3 case studies in depth. Make every one count.
